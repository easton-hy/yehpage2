里程碑

  1. F1 visa:
    • Check your I20, passport, photo, schedule appointment, SEVIS payment, student pass outside.
    • Don't bring laptop or other big electric equipment. If you bring, you need to 2 one dollar coins to storage your package.
    • Go inside, and check the doc again.
    • Give your passport and doc to officer, and take down a fingerprint. 4 fingers right, 4 fingers left, and two thumbs.
    • Wait for the interview.
    • Give your doc and passport to the office and ask the questions, as shown in 240129.
    • Blue paper means approve, and the yellow paper mean be checked or need to submit other material.
    • I was given yellow paper, and saying "my application required additional administrative processing, and no further action required from you. Once the processing is complete, we will email you with further instructions."
